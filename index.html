About Me

Hey, guys, I am Feihong, a typical literary youth, love Sci-Fi, sketching, and traveling.
I'm currently a master's student of Ghent University in Statistical data analysis, and my thesis project is involved with bioinformatics. Persuing a Master's degree in foreign countries during the epidemic might not be a wise decision, but I never regret to make such decisions. I super miss my life in Xi'an but also love my life in Ghent. The most important and happiest thing for me is I met my Mr.Charlie hero here ❤ (kiss my idiot).
Before coming to Belgium, I ran my own business with serveral friends in China, mainly involved with education and information technology. I set up a company with friends called Zhengzhou Sula Felinae Computer Technology Co., Ltd at the beginning of 2016, which aimed to solve the problem of present-day college students’ social life. And I was mainly responsible for technical issues and business analysis. After this business, I built up a group called Dragon on the Tower Education Technology Group, which aimed to help students apply for their dream schools. I have to say that our business is pretty successful and I feel lucky to have such excellent and reliable teammates!
I also worked in Prof.Li Xiaoge's group called Text Mining during my undergraduate study. My research interests focus on data analysis (via machine learning methods). During working in the TextMining Lab, I participated in three projects which were related to Sentiment Analysis and/or Knowledge Graph.
My hometown lies in the north part of China, and I persued my bachelor's degree also in the north part of China. My friends, however, from all over the world.
All these experiences give me the ability to adapt to a multicultural environment and have a high level of intellectual curiosity.
